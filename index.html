1. Paneer Tikka

    Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Spices: 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala,
    1 tsp cumin powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 bell pepper, 1 onion, 1 tomato (all cut into squares)
  • Skewers

    Instructions:

  1. Marinade: Mix yogurt, gram flour, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, spices, and oil.
  2. Marinate: Coat paneer and veggies with marinade. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer: Thread paneer and veggies onto skewers.
  4. Cook: Grill or bake at 200°C (392°F) for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally.
  5. Serve: Garnish with lemon wedges and serve with mint chutney.

2. Momos

    Ingredients:

  • Dough: 2 cups all-purpose flour, pinch of salt, water
  • Filling: 1 cup chopped veggies, 1/2 cup chopped onions, 2-3 minced garlic cloves, 1-inch grated ginger,
    1-2 chopped green chilies, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oil, salt, pepper, optional minced meat

    Instructions:

  1. Dough: Mix flour and salt, add water, knead, and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Filling: Sauté onions, garlic, ginger, and chilies in oil. Add veggies and soy sauce, cook until
    3. tender.
  3. Assemble: Roll dough into wrappers, fill, and seal.
  4. Steam: Steam momos for 10-12 minutes until cooked.

3. Mutton Biryani

    Ingredients:

  • Mutton pieces
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions, tomatoes, yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaves)
  • Garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder
  • Fresh herbs (mint and coriander leaves)
  • Ghee or oil
  • Saffron milk (optional)
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

    Instructions:

  1. Marinate Mutton: Mix mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Marinate for 1-2 hours.
  2. Cook Rice: Partially cook rice with whole spices until 70% done.
  3. Cook Mutton: Fry onions, add marinated mutton, tomatoes, and cook until tender.
  4. Layer and Dum: Layer mutton and rice in a pot, add herbs, saffron milk, and lemon juice. Seal and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.

4. Chicken Pizza

    Ingredients:

  • Dough: 2 cups flour, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 cup warm water, 1 tsp yeast
  • Toppings: 1 cup cooked chicken, 1 cup pizza sauce, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, sliced onions,
    bell peppers, black olives, dried oregano, dried basil

    Instructions:

  1. Dough: Dissolve sugar and yeast in warm water. Mix with flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat Oven: 220°C (425°F).
  3. Prepare Toppings: Cook chicken and slice veggies.
  4. Assemble Pizza: Roll out dough, add sauce, half cheese, chicken, veggies, remaining cheese, oregano, and basil.
  5. Bake: 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

5. Fish Fry

    Ingredients:

  • Fish fillets
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour, cornmeal, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder
  • Egg, milk
  • Oil for frying
  • Lemon wedges

    Instructions:

  1. Season: Fish with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat: Dip in flour mixture, egg mixture, then flour again.
  3. Fry: In hot oil until golden brown, 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Serve: With lemon wedges.

6. Cake

    Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

    Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare Pan: Grease and flour a 9-inch round baking pan.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Beat Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  5. Combine: Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth.
  6. Bake: Pour batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
